How to share your family tree

To give someone access to your family:

  1. Click Manage Access on any family card below.
  2. Enter their email address and choose a role.
  3. They sign in with Google using that same email and will see your family automatically.
Editor Can add & edit people
Viewer Can browse but not edit

Everyone with access sees exactly the same family data.

How families work

A family is a named group — usually a last name like "Smith". Create the family first, then add members to it.

You can belong to multiple families (e.g. if you married into another family or manage two family groups).

Everyone you grant access to will see all members of that family across Search, the Tree, and the Calendar.
